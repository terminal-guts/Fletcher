## Research Findings

### GREAT CRITERIA (max 12 points)

**1. Moat / Hard to Replicate: __/2**
[What protects this firm from competition? Can a well-funded competitor catch up in 3 years? Evidence: specific methodologies, relationships, certifications, client lock-in?]

**2. Principals Are Practitioners First: __/2**
[Do leaders do the work? Do they have specific opinions about craft? Evidence: project credits, award language, interview tone, social presence showing hands-on involvement?]

**3. Retention & Referral: __/2**
[Do clients come back? Evidence: repeated clients in portfolio over 5+ years, multiple projects per client, "repeat customer" language?]

**4. Right But Unpopular Decisions: __/2**
[Have they made a conviction-driven bet against industry consensus? Evidence: award citations mentioning "innovative approach," interviews discussing counterintuitive choices?]

**5. Consistent Culture Top to Bottom: __/2**
[Do junior staff describe firm the same way principals do? Evidence: Glassdoor reviews, employee testimonials, social media from non-principals, low turnover?]

**6. They Say No: __/2**
[Do they turn down work that doesn't fit? Evidence: gaps in portfolio (possible refusals), confident pricing, interviews discussing declined work?]

**GREAT SUBTOTAL: __/12**

### UNDERAPPRECIATED CRITERIA (max 12 points)

**1. Weak Public Presence Relative to Work Quality: __/2**
[Is their website dated despite excellent work? Sparse social media? Low press outreach? Evidence: website design/update frequency, Google mentions, award entry frequency?]

**2. Known Locally, Invisible Broadly: __/2**
[Are they the "go-to" in one region but unknown nationally? Evidence: all project references from one state/city, local awards only, zero national visibility?]

**3. Referral-Dependent But Not Saturated: __/2**
[Do they have capacity to grow? Evidence: "We have room to take on more work," capacity >80%, slow growth, not on radar of large institutional buyers?]

**4. Peer Recognition Exceeds Market Recognition: __/2**
[Do industry insiders respect them while broader market sleeps? Evidence: multiple AIA/chapter awards, cited by peer firms, featured in trade publications, no corporate/marketing visibility?]

**5. Pricing Doesn't Match Quality: __/2**
[Is there a fee gap? Evidence: doing premium work at mid-market pricing, "we could charge more," undercutting peers despite superior quality?]

**6. Recent Transition Creates Window: __/2**
[Has something recently changed that creates an opportunity? Evidence: leadership change, new service line, office expansion, website updates, LinkedIn announcements?]

**UNDERAPPRECIATED SUBTOTAL: __/12**
