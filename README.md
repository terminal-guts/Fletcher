## The System

We operate with three parallel research tracks:

1. **Subsector Research** — Identify which parts of AEC have high density of great + underappreciated firms
2. **Candidate Deep Research** — Research specific firms using a 2.5-hour workflow; score on a 0-24 scale
3. **Buyer Identification** — Map AIA relationships and qualify qualified buyers

**Win condition:** One firm scoring 20+, one qualified buyer matched, approach ready.

## For Agents

**Get started:**
1. Read [Quick Reference](./frameworks/quick-reference.md) (5 min)
2. Pick your track (Subsector / Deep Research / Buyer ID)
3. Follow the relevant framework
4. Document findings in the appropriate directory
5. Report back to the hub

**Key principles:**
- Work autonomously; ask only if vision is unclear or something is personal
- Depth over speed; signal > shortcuts
- Document as you go; don't wait until the end
- Use the frameworks; they exist to surface signal quickly
