## Engagement Model

**Duration:** 2-3 months (typical range: 6-12 weeks)

**Team Size:** 2-3 people from Saul's team

**Phases:**

1. **Discovery (1-2 weeks)**
   - Fully understand the blocking factor
   - Interview founder, team, customers
   - Document current state and root causes
   - Define success metrics

2. **Design (1-2 weeks)**
   - Propose solution (process, system, tool, organizational change)
   - Get founder approval
   - Build detailed implementation plan

3. **Build/Execute (4-12 weeks)**
   - Implement with the company's team
   - Train them as we go
   - Weekly check-ins, course corrections
   - Focus on transferring knowledge, not being indispensable

4. **Transition (2-4 weeks)**
   - Document everything
   - Ensure team can operate without us
   - Gradual step-back over 2-4 weeks
   - Ongoing availability for questions post-engagement

## What's Included in the Fee

✅ Discovery interviews and documentation
✅ Design and planning
✅ Build/implementation and training
✅ Transition support (2-4 weeks)

❌ Capital investment (we're not investors)
❌ Ongoing management/operations

---

## Success Metrics

**We define success together in Discovery, typically:**

- Blocking factor significantly reduced or solved
- Company team can iterate/maintain the solution without us
- Financial impact realized (revenue growth, margin improvement, time savings, etc.)
- Relationship: Would founder want to work with us again? Would they refer us?

---

## Cancellation & Adjustment

- **If we discover the blocking factor is unsolvable:** We discuss terminating early and refund appropriately
- **If scope changes significantly:** We renegotiate fee and timeline
- **If timeline extends:** We renegotiate (premium for additional weeks)

---

## What Happens After Engagement

**Month 1-3 Post-Engagement:**
- We're available for questions (included in original fee)
- Company owns and iterates
- We step back completely

**Month 4+:**
- Relationship is ongoing but not active
- If founder wants continued advisory: Can renegotiate
- If company hits new blocking factor: New engagement / new fee
